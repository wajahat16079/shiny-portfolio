DWRAP Refugees Policy Analysis

Developed for World Bank
Data Analysis Policy Interactive R Shiny

The "Dataset of World Refugee and Asylum Policies (DWRAP)" offers a complete dataset of de jure asylum and refugee policies for all 193 countries for the 70 years from 1951-2022. The DWRAP index aggregates policies from 5 fields: Access; Services; Livelihoods; Movement, and Citizenship and Participation.

Philippines Portfolio Analysis & Impact Assessment

Developed for World Bank
Spatial Analysis Economic Development Impact Evaluation R Shiny

This geospatial tool implements the World Bank's Project Targeting Index (PTI) for the Philippines, helping optimize resource allocation across provinces. Users can build custom targeting indices using indicators for poverty, human capital, labor, and social services. The dashboard identifies areas with greatest potential impact, analyzes correlations between development indicators, and compares regions with and without existing projects. Note: Please allow 30-50 seconds for the application to load.
